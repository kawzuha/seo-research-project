## Full Content / Transcript

AI Content for SEO: common scaled content pitfalls and 3 strategies to avoid them.

Pitfalls:
- Publishing AI content without briefs or SERP analysis
- No brand voice consistency across scaled output
- Missing E-E-A-T signals and human expertise overlay
- Bulk publishing without editorial review

3 Strategies:
1. Brief-first production: never generate without SERP-informed briefs
2. Brand voice layer: fine-tuned personas or detailed style guides
3. Human-in-the-loop: mandatory review at brief, draft, and pre-publish stages

Advocates agentic SEO—single-purpose agents for brief generation, drafting, optimization, and publishing rather than one-shot ChatGPT prompts.
