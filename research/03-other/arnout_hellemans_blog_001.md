## Full Content / Transcript

Arnout Hellemans on quality over AI content volume in SEO 2025.

Key message: Stop churning out AI-generated content at volume. Focus on query satisfaction and content that genuinely completes user tasks.

Quality control principles:
- Every piece must satisfy the search query completely
- AI volume production without quality review damages brand authority
- Intent research before content production, not keyword volume
- Engagement metrics as quality signals (dwell time, task completion)
- Human editorial judgment cannot be replaced by AI scaling
