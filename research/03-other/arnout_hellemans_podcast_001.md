## Full Content / Transcript

Podcast on SEO 2025 focusing on task completion and query satisfaction.

Content production framework:
- Define task completion criteria before writing
- Map content to specific user tasks, not keyword buckets
- Quality assurance: does the content fully satisfy the query?
- AI can assist research and drafting but human must validate task completion
- Engagement metrics validate whether content actually satisfies queries

Production QA process: write → task completion check → engagement monitoring → iterate.
