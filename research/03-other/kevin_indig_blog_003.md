## Full Content / Transcript

Brand-first SEO era discussion with Kevin Indig on when AI-generated content works.

When AI content works:
- Templated, data-driven content with clear structure
- Content where brand voice is established via fine-tuning or detailed briefs
- Supplementary content supporting core brand pillars
- Content with strong human editorial overlay

When it fails:
- Generic informational content without brand differentiation
- Content lacking first-hand experience signals
- Mass-produced content without editorial governance
