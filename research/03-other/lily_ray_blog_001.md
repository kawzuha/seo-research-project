## Full Content / Transcript

Reflection on how SEO, GEO, and AI search evolved in 2025.

Key themes for content production:
- AI has leveled the technological playing field, raising the bar for truly helpful, original content
- Search engines and AI assistants will continue promoting content with real human expertise, opinions, and authenticity
- Content that can be mass-produced with minimal effort faces increasing quality scrutiny
- The fundamental Google approach (effort, originality, E-E-A-T) remains unchanged despite AI tooling
- Distribution across multiple surfaces (web, YouTube, UGC) matters for AI retrieval corpus

Content production implication: invest in genuinely differentiated content that AI cannot replicate, using AI only for workflow efficiency.
