## Full Content / Transcript

Kevin Indig discusses automation in SEO for 2025, emphasizing AI drafts combined with programmatic content enhancements.

Key workflow insights:
- Use AI to generate initial content drafts at scale (0→80% automation)
- Apply programmatic enhancements at the page level for structured data and templates
- Human editorial review remains essential for quality and brand alignment
- Batch content production: generate 30 drafts per quarter, editors polish top performers
- Programmatic SEO works best for templated, data-driven page types

Production system: AI draft generation → programmatic page enhancements → human editorial review → publish → monitor decay.
