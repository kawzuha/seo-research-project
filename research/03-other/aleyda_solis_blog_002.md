## Full Content / Transcript

Podcast interview covering how AI is transforming SEO while core principles remain.

Key content production insights from Aleyda:
- Uses LLMs to speed up analysis, surface patterns, and enhance productivity without reinventing the wheel.
- Chunk-based content strategies: optimize at passage/chunk level for AI retrieval, not just page level.
- Continue investing in informational content but be mindful—must be on-brand, not fluffy glossary content.
- Recommended on-brand informational types: industry insights, research stats, trends, case studies, thought leadership, comparisons, FAQs from community, help centers.
- Think topics rather than individual prompts for AI visibility tracking.
- Assess visibility by product line/topic context (e.g., women's sneakers vs generic brand visibility).
- Persona-driven content prioritization over raw search volume from tools.
- Align with customer support, product, digital PR, and social teams when selecting content topics.

On AI workflow: leverage existing SEO expertise for new LLM channels rather than creating separate GEO teams prematurely.
