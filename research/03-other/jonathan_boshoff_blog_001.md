## Full Content / Transcript

Jonathan Boshoff's 12-step process for scalable AI content for SEO:

1. Define content pillars and topic clusters
2. Run SERP analysis for target keywords
3. Generate AI content briefs with competitor analysis
4. Create fine-tuned persona or brand voice guidelines
5. Generate AI draft using brief + persona
6. Run E-E-A-T comparison against Google Quality Rater Guidelines
7. Add human expertise overlay (original data, case studies, experience)
8. Optimize with NLP analysis for entity coverage
9. Internal linking via semantic retrieval
10. Schema markup and FAQ sections
11. Human editorial review (read-aloud test)
12. Publish and monitor performance

Each step has quality gates—content cannot skip human review stages.
