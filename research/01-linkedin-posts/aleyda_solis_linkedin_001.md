✅ Right take: "Fluff informational content disconnected from the real brand subject matter expertise that doesn't connect with the potential customer needs and/or doesn't add anything new and useful for them is Dead"

Informational Content that is actually written with real expertise and experience, addressing real needs of your target audience, solving their doubts and issues through their search journey, is still critical to establish your brand authority for not only traditional search, but also LLMs!

This is what your people will refer, link to and take into account in their decision making process when converting. Continue writing it with the right, non-fluff approach and process.
