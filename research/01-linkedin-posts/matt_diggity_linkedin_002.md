How to write content optimized for Google's AI Overviews:

- Use plain language over keyword stuffing
- Answer questions directly in standalone paragraphs
- Structure content in extractable chunks AI can summarize
- Include FAQ sections with specific user questions
- Lead with the answer, then provide supporting detail
- Use natural conversational tone (read-aloud test)

Content production teams should format every page for both human readers and AI extraction.
