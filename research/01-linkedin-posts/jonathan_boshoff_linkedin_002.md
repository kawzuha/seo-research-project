## Full Content / Transcript

I Fine-tuned a chat model with my blog content and now it writes just like me. I also fine-tuned a chat model using SEMrush blogs and it's 91% similar in terms of wording and writing style to the original writing.

With the new Personas feature in Moonlit, I can easily create a fine-tuned chat model based on any website's content. When you fine-tune a chat model, you don't need to specify the writing style and personality, it just intuitively knows.

Fine-tuning works better than instructions because it changes the weighting of words based on the training data. I can still mix and match for better results: combining fine-tuned models, prompting, a knowledgebase, and weave in other apps like SERP scraping and Google NLP analysis.

Setup: provide sitemap (up to 50 pages) → prepare training data → start training → use persona in any chat model app. Can combine with knowledge base (Google Quality Guidelines) for E-E-A-T checks.

Comparison: fine-tuned persona 91% similarity vs base model 76% similarity to original SEMrush blog post.
