## Full Content / Transcript

The biggest SEO lie of 2025? "AI content can't rank anymore."

My agency helped a client go from 913 to 8,291 organic monthly visitors using AI content. But we didn't just ask ChatGPT to write articles and upload.

**Step 1: Topic clustering > Keyword research**
- Start with 5–7 core topics
- Use ChatGPT to generate 30+ semantically relevant subtopics per core
- Break each subtopic into keyword variations by intent

**Step 2: AI content needs human expertise (70/30 rule)**
- 70% AI-generated base content
- 30% human expertise overlay (or SurferAI)
- Add: original data, personal experiences, client case studies, industry insights

**Step 3: First-hand experience**
- Specific practitioner details, screenshots, custom visuals, documented tests

**Step 4: E-E-A-T signals**
- Author credentials, timestamped facts, industry citations, SME quotes

**Step 5: Formatting for quality**
- Custom subheadings, data visualization, FAQ sections, schema markup
